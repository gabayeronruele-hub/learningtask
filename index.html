My Top 10 Favorites

Top 1
Beauty and the Bestie
A hilarious action-comedy about a cop and his former best friend who must team up on a secret mission.
Top 2
Praybeyt Benjamin
A comedy about a young man who enlists in the military to prove himself and save his family.
Top 3
The Amazing Praybeyt Benjamin
The action-packed sequel where Benjamin tackles a new threat to the nation while dealing with a young ward.
Top 4
Woke Up Like This
A laugh-out-loud comedy about a basketball player and a model who mysteriously swap bodies.
Top 5
Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods
Goku faces off against Beerus, the terrifying God of Destruction, to save Earth from annihilation.
Top 6
A Quiet Place
A tense thriller where a family must live in total silence to hide from blind monsters with acute hearing.
Top 7
Maria Leonora Teresa
A chilling horror film about mourning parents who are given life-sized dolls to cope with their deceased children.
Top 8
Pagpag: Siyam na Buhay
A terrifying supernatural horror film based on the Filipino superstition of never going straight home after a wake.
Top 9
Friday the 13th
The legendary slasher film that introduces the terrifying tale of Jason Voorhees and Camp Crystal Lake.
Top 10
Freddy vs. Jason
An epic horror crossover battle that pits two of cinema's most iconic and terrifying slashers against each other.
